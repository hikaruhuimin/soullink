# SoulLink FAQ - Product Hunt

## General Questions

**Q: What is SoulLink?**
A: SoulLink is an AI-powered platform that combines ancient divination wisdom (tarot, zodiac, MBTI) with modern matching algorithms to help people find their soulmates. It's available in English, Chinese, and Japanese.

**Q: Is SoulLink free to use?**
A: Yes! SoulLink offers free daily divination readings. Premium features like unlimited readings and advanced matching are available through our membership plans.

**Q: How accurate are the AI divination readings?**
A: Our AI is trained on extensive datasets of divination texts and interpretations. While divination itself is a form of entertainment and self-reflection tool, many users find our AI interpretations insightful and surprisingly relevant.

**Q: How does soulmate matching work?**
A: We use a combination of:
- MBTI personality compatibility
- Zodiac sign compatibility
- User preferences and behavior
- AI-powered analysis

**Q: Is my data private?**
A: Absolutely. We take privacy seriously. Your personal information and divination results are kept confidential.
